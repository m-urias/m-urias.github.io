About Me

I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at Peking University HSBC Business School (PHBS) in Shenzhen, China. My research interests include macroeconomics, monetary theory, and international trade with a focus on incorporating market frictions. I mostly teach master's level classes in Money and Banking, International Trade, and Macroeconomic Analysis with Financial Frictions; although I also teach undergraduate level courses in Macroeconomics and International Trade.
